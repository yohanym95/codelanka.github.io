Our Background and Mission

What We Are

There are talented people collaborated together with us in creating, developing, designing and making novel things using Open Technologies, Open Data and software. Those experiences of our work are to be shared for the benefit of all. Further, Open Tech Meetups, Camps and Workshops are being organized for freshers by expert developers, designers and contributers.p>

Why We Do It

Open technologies and data solutions procure the society to do creative and innovative things while providing eady access, free of charge. By sharing these technologies, we hope to see the positive impact as well as the improvement that information technology and science can make on human lifestyle, which is our prime goal.

Who We Are

We desire to witness the progress and development of the livelihood of Asians as an organization based in Asia. Not stopping there, as an organization consists of people from all continents, we hope to continue this worldwide too. Our main targets are to support people to figure out the best solutions to the critical problems that arise in our societies and to set an easy access to data, without any hindrance.

What We Do

Enable Open Data

We encourage community to use and make open data and make cool things using Open data.

Empower Open Source

We produce 100% opensource solutions for critical problems that we found in the community.

Empower Community

We organise code camps, workshops, meetups coloborate with other organisations such as Google Developer Group, FOSS.lk
